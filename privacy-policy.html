Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Money Mastery ("we," "our," or "the app") is a financial literacy education game developed for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Money Mastery does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress and data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any external server.

Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to: names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

Third-Party Services

Money Mastery does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Data Storage

All game progress, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Money Mastery is designed to be suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

In-App Purchases

Money Mastery does not currently include any in-app purchases. If this changes in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
