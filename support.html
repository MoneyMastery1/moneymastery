FAQ

Is Money Mastery really free?
Yes. No ads, no in-app purchases, no hidden costs. Free forever.
Does the app collect my data?
No. Money Mastery works entirely offline. We don't collect, store, or share any personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
What devices does it work on?
Money Mastery is available for iPhone running iOS 17 or later.
Do I need an internet connection?
No. The entire app works offline once downloaded.
I'm stuck on a level. Any tips?
Pay attention to Theo's feedback after each question — he explains the reasoning behind every answer. You need 2+ stars to unlock the next game, so focus on accuracy over speed.
How do I beat the boss battles?
Boss battles test you across all the games in that world. Review Theo's tips from earlier games, keep your streak going, and don't let Brax get in your head.
Can I reset my progress?
Currently there's no reset button, but this feature is coming in a future update.
How do I report a bug?
Email us at moneymastery1@gmail.com with a description of what happened, what screen you were on, and a screenshot if possible. We'll fix it fast.
